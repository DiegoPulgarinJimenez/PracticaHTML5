Paint: Les Amants, René Magritte, 1928

Technique: Oleo

Two people without identity are kissing behind two wet veils. A secret love ...? A forbidden love…? Two strangers who like each other without knowing each other ...? The insensitivity of some loves ...? Blind love ...? The surrealist Magritte explains nothing. He only leaves us that feeling of suffocation, which is perhaps a symptom like any other to illustrate love.

Paint: Les Joueurs de cartes,Paul Cézanne, 1890-1895

Technique: Oleo

Les Joueurs de cartes is a series of oil paintings by the French Post-Impressionist artist Paul Cézanne. Painted during Cézanne's final period in the early 1890s, there are five paintings in the series.
